About Me

As a front-end developer, I combine creativity and technology to build dynamic, user-friendly websites. With expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and WordPress, I create responsive and engaging designs. I also integrate tools like Node.js and GitHub to streamline workflows and ensure high-performance websites.


Recently, I have developed a strong growing interest in data analysis, particularly in the healthcare sector. I am actively self-learning Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Jupyter Notebooks to explore and interpret health data. My goal is to leverage data-driven insights to improve user experiences and contribute to meaningful projects.

Advocacy for Neurodiversity and Inclusivity

As a neurodiverse developer, I’m deeply passionate about creating technology that is inclusive and accessible for all. My own experiences shape my approach to design, and I strive to build websites and applications that embrace diverse cognitive needs and perspectives. By adhering to accessibility standards and designing with inclusivity in mind, I aim to create digital experiences that are user-friendly and welcoming for everyone.

Climate Tech and sustainability

My journey in front-end development aligns with my growing commitment to sustainability and climate tech. I’m a firm believer that technology has a pivotal role in tackling climate challenges. By integrating eco-conscious practices into web development—like performance optimization to minimize energy consumption—I aim to align my work with environmental responsibility.
